Stevie

Stevie is a rehabilitating toy that children can engage with by using repetitive motions to set of soothing sensory feedback through music.

ROLE

Research, Strategy, Visual Design, Coding

TEAM

Linda Shu, Jenny Nguyen

CONTEXT

Stevie was a four week project for a 2nd year Interactive Systems course. For this project, we were required to create an interactive object that utilized three or more sensors and required the use of Arduino and Processing.

Research

We noted that the popularity of fidget toys increased in 2017 and were urged into researching the effectiveness of the product. We discovered that the repetitive spinning motion satisfies the senses, engaging in tactile and visual stimulation. By creating a simple continuous motion, these sensory devices are found to calm and help focus a child with autism by occupying nervous hands and allowing coordinated, predictable movements.
